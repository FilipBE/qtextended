Video call manager

This feature is provided as part of the Deskphone Reference Design.

The local video camera can be turned on and off at any time during an active VoIP call. Depending on acceptance and capability of the recipient, streamed video from the local camera is sent to the recipient and displayed on their device.

If the remote camera is turned on, the received video is shown in place of their avatar, or user image.

The video from either caller or recipient can be shown in full screen.

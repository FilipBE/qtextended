Edit Contacts

Four information tabs are available for recording different contact details.

Use / buttons Press tabs to select Contact, Business, Personal or Media pages.

Contact:

  • Name: shows on contact list.
  • Emails: email addresses.
  • Business contact: turn on to show company and title on this screen and add the contact to the Business group.
  • Company: company name.
  • Title: job title.
  • Phone numbers: add as many as you want.
  • Phone number Type: select Type from the Options menu.

Business: Company, Title show same as on Contact page. Phone, Mobile, Fax, Pager also show the same business numbers as set on the Contact page.

Personal: Phone, Mobile, Fax show same home numbers as set on Contact page.

Media:

  • Photo: shows on Contacts list and shows when this person calls you. To add or change a photo, highlight and select.
  • Ringtone: will sound when this person calls.
  • Videotone: will be shown when this person calls.
  • Notes: use this to add free-text notes, including hyperlinks to other Contacts, Calendar or Tasks records.
  • Groups: used to group this contact to similar records.

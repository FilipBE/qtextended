Account Settings

Before you can send and retrieve MMS and email messages you must have a GPRS network connection. To send and retrieve MMS messages an MMS account must be created, to send and retrieve email messages an email account must be created. Normally you would only need one MMS account for MMS messages and one email account for email messages. Note: You can setup one MMS account and as many email accounts as required.

To create or edit an account:

  • Press Options and select Account Settings... from the menu.
  • Select an existing account to edit or press Options and select Add account... from the menu.
  • Enter the account details into the fields on the tabs.
  • When you have finished configuring your account, press Back to save your settings.

To remove an account:

  • Press Options and select Account Settings... from the menu.
  • Highlight the account, press Options and select Remove account... from the menu.

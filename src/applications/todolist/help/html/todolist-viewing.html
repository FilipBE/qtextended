Viewing Tasks

To view a list of all the tasks, simply open the Tasks application.

View Tasks by Category:

  • Press Options and select View Category... from the menu.
  • In the list of categories select the categories you wish to view.
  • A line at the bottom of the list of tasks will display your current category selection.
  • The next time you launch Tasks your last selected categories will be displayed.

Viewing a single Task

Selecting a task from the list of tasks will present you with the details of the task, including any progress, recurrence or reminder information. If there is more than one task in your current list of tasks, you can press the or arrow buttons to choose the previous or next tasks.

Mine Hunt

Objective: quickly find all the squares that don't contain mines.

Use to move the highlight, then press the Select button to discover if there is a mine under the tile. The numbers show how many mines are under the eight surrounding tiles. It's up to you to guess where.

Hint: Press the * button or hold down the Select button to flag tiles that you are certain contain a mine.

Press on a tile to discover whether there is a mine under the tile. The numbers show how many mines are under the eight surrounding tiles. It's up to you to guess where.

Hint: Press and hold the stylus to flag tiles that you are certain contain a mine.

Mine hunt can be played at beginner, advanced or expert level.

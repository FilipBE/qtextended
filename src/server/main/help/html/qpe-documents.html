Documents

Documents such as Video and Audio clips, Notes, Pictures and Voice Notes can be created and/or viewed with various applications installed on your device.

To view your documents:

  • Open the launcher and select to launch Documents.

To open a document with the application used to create it: highlight the document in the list and press Select to select the document. tap on the document in the list.

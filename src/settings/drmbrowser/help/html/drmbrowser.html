Licenses

The Licenses application allows you to manange installed licenses for protected content.

OMA DRM Licenses

The OMA DRM Licenses tab provides a list of all installed licenses for OMA DRM protected content on your device. Selecting a license from the main list will display the permissions it can be rendered with, their current validity and any constraints placed on their use.

A selected license can be deleted by pressing Options and selecting Delete license option from the menu. Protected content is only accessible while there are one or more valid licenses remaining for it.

OMA DRM Settings

OMA DRM Settings

The OMA DRM Settings tab enables optional features of OMA DRM

Enabling Silent rights acquisition allows your to acquire licenses for some protected content without prompting first

Transaction tracking embeds information identifying your in protected content you distribute to other people, this information is provided to content providers when a recipient attempts to acquire a license.

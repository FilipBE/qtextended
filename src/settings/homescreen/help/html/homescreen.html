Homescreen Settings

The Homescreen application is used to set the appearance and behavior of your homescreen.

Appearance tab

  • Image: set an image to be used as your homescreen image. Once an image has been set, you can choose to display it Scale & Crop, Stretch, Tile, Center, or Scale.
  • Display: choose what information will be displayed on your homescreen.

Idle tab

Set whether you want to automatically return to the homescreen On display off, On suspend, or Never. You can also choose to lock the device when automatically returning to the homescreen.

Secondary tab

This tab presents the same settings as the Appearance tab, but these settings will be applied to the secondary screen.
